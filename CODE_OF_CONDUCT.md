# Code of Conduct

ActaCofrade adopts the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) as its code of conduct. The text below is a human adaptation of it, written to read naturally and reflect the tone of the project.

## Our commitment

Those of us who maintain and contribute to this project are committed to making participation a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, physical appearance, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sexual characteristics, gender identity or expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We want a healthy community where people can disagree without being disrespectful, and where learning in public feels comfortable.

## What this looks like day to day

**Behaviours that fit this project:**

- Showing empathy and kindness to others.
- Respecting opinions, viewpoints and experiences different from your own.
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback in good faith.
- Taking responsibility for your own mistakes: apologising if you caused harm and learning from the experience.
- Looking for what is best not just for one person, but for the community.

**Behaviours that are not acceptable:**

- Use of sexualised language or images, or any unwanted sexual attention or advances.
- Dismissive comments, insults, personal or political attacks directed at others.
- Public or private harassment.
- Publishing private information about others (physical address, email, etc.) without their explicit permission.
- Any other behaviour that would be considered inappropriate in a professional environment.

## Responsibility

The person who maintains the project is responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct, and will take fair and proportionate corrective action in response to any behaviour they consider inappropriate, threatening, offensive or harmful.

This includes the right to edit, delete or reject comments, commits, code, issues, pull requests or other contributions that do not align with this code, as well as the possibility of banning participation, temporarily or permanently, from anyone who adopts behaviour considered inappropriate, threatening, offensive or harmful.

## Scope

This code applies in all project spaces: the GitHub repository, issues, pull requests, associated chat channels, and social media mentions when acting on behalf of the project.

## How to report an incident

If you witness or experience unacceptable behaviour, write directly to the person who maintains the project (GitHub profile: [@Aranaaa00](https://github.com/Aranaaa00)). All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and confidentially.

Whoever receives the report is required to respect the privacy and safety of the person reporting.

## Enforcement

For minor incidents, a private conversation will be held with the person involved and any necessary apologies or corrections will be requested.

For repeated or serious incidents, possible measures include a public warning, temporary suspension from participation in the project, and in the most severe cases, a permanent ban.
